About

A life devoted to compassion,
creativity, and conscious living.

I am a vegan activist, artist, translator of sacred scriptures, spiritual counsellor, culinary creator, and founder of purpose-driven initiatives rooted in empathy. Through Voice for the Paws and Happy Kitchen, I create spaces where kindness becomes action.

My work bridges spiritual depth and artistic expression, bringing healing, awareness, and beauty into everyday life. Every creation is an act of devotion: to the earth, to all beings, and to the boundless capacity of the human heart.
